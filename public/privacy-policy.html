Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 4, 2025

Properties Thailand ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services in connection with the Copacabana Coral Reef development.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Contact us via WhatsApp, phone, or email
  • Request property information, floor plans, or brochures
  • Subscribe to our newsletter or updates
  • Use our interactive tools (unit finder, investment calculator, quiz)
  • Register for property viewings or consultations

This information may include: name, email address, phone number, WhatsApp number, country of residence, investment budget, property preferences, and any other information you choose to provide.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing activities, including:

  • IP address and general location data
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referral source (how you arrived at our website)
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Click patterns and navigation paths

1.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and analyze website performance. This includes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Marketing Cookies: Facebook Pixel and other tools to deliver relevant advertising

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling certain cookies may limit website functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide requested information
  • Send property brochures, floor plans, and pricing details
  • Schedule and confirm property viewings
  • Provide personalized property recommendations
  • Process and manage property transactions
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Improve our website and services
  • Analyze user behavior and preferences
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

3. Facebook Pixel and Advertising

We use Facebook Pixel and similar advertising technologies to:

  • Track conversions from Facebook ads
  • Build custom audiences for retargeting
  • Optimize ad delivery to interested users
  • Measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns

Facebook may use this data for its own purposes, including providing measurement services and targeted ads. For more information, see Facebook's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of targeted advertising through:

  • Facebook Ad Preferences
  • Your Online Choices (EU)
  • Your device settings (iOS/Android)

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Copacabana Group: The property developer, to process your inquiries and facilitate purchases
  • Legal Advisors: To provide conveyancing and legal services
  • Service Providers: Third-party companies that assist with marketing, analytics, and customer service
  • Business Partners: Mortgage brokers, property management companies, and other service providers (with your consent)
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights

We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. When your information is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal information
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence, including Thailand, the United Kingdom, and the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

10. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (including Copacabana Group's official website and Matterport 3D tours). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of our services after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
