7. Thai Property Law Considerations

7.1 Foreign Ownership

Foreign nationals may be subject to restrictions when purchasing property in Thailand, including:

  • Maximum 49% foreign quota for condominium buildings
  • Proof of funds brought into Thailand in foreign currency
  • Compliance with Foreign Exchange Transaction Forms
  • Restrictions on land ownership (land lease structures may apply)

7.2 Transfer Fees and Taxes

Property purchases in Thailand typically incur additional costs, including:

  • Transfer fees (approximately 2% of registered value)
  • Stamp duty (0.5%)
  • Withholding tax (may apply)
  • Business tax (may apply for resales within 5 years)

Buyer and seller may negotiate responsibility for these costs.
